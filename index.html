FAQ

Can I train if I'm a beginner?

Of course, our professional coaches work with tennis players of all levels of training, including novice players. We will help you learn the basics of tennis and develop your skills

Who can attend the events?

Our events are open to all club members and their guests. We welcome players of all skill levels and ages to participate in our various tennis events.

What types of courts are there?

We offer various court surfaces including hard courts, clay courts, and indoor courts. All our courts are professionally maintained and available for both practice and competitive play.

How often are the events held?

We organize regular events throughout the year, including weekly club matches, monthly tournaments, and special seasonal events. Check our events calendar for specific dates and details.
